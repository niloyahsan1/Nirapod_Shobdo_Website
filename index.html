How Nirapod Shobdo Works

Multiple ways to report cases and request assistance

Reporting System

1. Open App

Launch Nirapod Shobdo app and select your preferred mode

2. Choose Report Mode

Select voice, video or visual icon based form to report issues

3. Submit Report

Send your report and receive confirmation with Tracking ID

Voice Reporting

Record your voice message to report issues instead of typing.

Video Reporting

Upload sign language videos to submit your reports.

Icon-Based Forms

Fill out visual icon form without needing to read or write complex text.
